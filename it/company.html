History

Since 1977 Best Mode has been a reference point in the distribution of quality women’s wear designed and manufactured in Italy.

For over thirty years of business Best Mode has been successful in updating its collections, following the market trends and changing its trade strategies according to its customers’ needs. Today Best Mode is a structured and flexible business, its staff is experienced and competent and it offers products and services tailor-made for every customer.

Mission

"To look for, produce, and distribute garments for all those who love the refined and elegant Italian style". This is Best Mode's mission.

In order to guarantee the highest quality Best Mode is constantly striving to achieve the maximum levels in terms of products, care for the services offered and technological innovation. Best Mode aims at preserving the trust relationship it has with its customers, and at remaining an ethical company which cares both for its customers' tastes and its commercial needs.

Partnership

Best Mode has partnerships with some of the most important businesses in this sector and cooperate with long standing workshops. The final product is also the result of our customers’ consultation and direct experience, because Best Mode is always in touch with the final customer.

Experience

Best Mode is led with experience and professionalism by Arturo Longhi, the founder and owner of the company. All the staff has been working in the business for years and has great experience. Moreover it regularly takes part in refresher courses, which are nowadays essential to operate on a constantly and quickly evolving market.

Solidarity

Best Mode is actively committed to the social sector. The social responsibility is deeply engraved in its DNA and it supports local charity organizations and bodies donating them garments, samples and prototypes.

Ethical Code

All the business activity is based on behavioural rules and ethical values aiming at protecting the environment and human resources. Family, health and safety are the pivotal principle in the company, as well as legality and absolute fairness in doing business.
